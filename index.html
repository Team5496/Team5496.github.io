About The Team

The RoboKnights are a FIRST robotics team from Vacaville, California. The team was created in 2014 by a small group of students and competed that year in Recycle Rush. In the years since, our team has continually improved and has ranked highly in several regional competitions. Our team is organised into four major departments: fabrication, electrical, programming, and PRB (PR and business). Along with these four departments, each season we vote on president, vice president, and project manager. We are always open to new members and encourage you to check out each department's page to find what might interest you most.
